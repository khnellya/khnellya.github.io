Jacqueline Monica Magi: Empowering Women's Voices Through Art and Advocacy

Jacqueline Monica Magi is a multifaceted individual who has transitioned from a career as a criminal judge in Pistoia to becoming a prolific writer, painter, fairy tale illustrator, and photographer. She holds the honorary position of President at the Anna Maria Marino Association "Against all violence," an organization actively engaged in combating gender violence, bullying, and homophobia.

The event "Voci di Donne," organized by the Direzione regionale musei della Toscana, Museo nazionale di Casa Giusti, Comune di Monsummano Terme, Associazione Amici di Casa Giusti, and Associazione Culturale e Teatrale Progetto IDRA, showcases Jacqueline Monica Magi's insights on "Bambine negate. Contro la violenza di genere."
